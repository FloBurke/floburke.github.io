My Top 3 Books

The Ballad Of Songbirds and Snakes

'The Ballad of Songbirds and Snakes' is the prequel to the Hunger Games trilogy in which Coriolanus Snow must mentor his female district 12 tribute, Lucy Grey Baird and help her survive the Hunger Games.

To Kill A Mockingbird

'To Kill a Mockingbird' is a classic novel that tells the tale of young Scout and Jem Finch and their childhood as their father Atticus is tasked with defending a black man accused of raping a white woman.

A Good Girls Guide To Murder

'A Good Girls Guide to Murder' is an amazing murder mystery book focusing on Pippa, a teenager who believes the story of Andie Bell's murder isn't true.
